Professional Values

Why does this section exist? Technical skills and experience are only part of the story. These values guide how I approach problems, work with teams, and craft solutions. They reflect lessons learned from both successes and failures throughout my journey.

  • Simplicity First: I believe the best solutions are often the simplest ones. Complex systems should be built from simple, well-understood components.
  • Practical Impact: I focus on delivering real business value rather than chasing technical perfection. The best code is the one that solves actual problems.
  • Code Elimination: Sometimes the best solution is no code at all. I've saved projects months of development by identifying existing solutions or simplifying requirements.
  • Continuous Evolution: Systems should be planned to evolve. I build for change, not for permanence.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Just as the Ankara community supported my early career, I believe in giving back through mentorship and community contribution.
